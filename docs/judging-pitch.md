# Scout.ai Judging Pitch

## Short Pitch

Scout.ai is an AI secret shopper for phone calls. It helps local business owners test whether staff convert inbound calls into customers by placing realistic AI mystery calls, analyzing the transcript, and producing a coaching report with the exact sentences that lost the sale.

## Why Owners Pay

Owners pay because missed calls and weak phone conversations quietly waste ad spend, referrals, and customer intent. Scout.ai turns that invisible leakage into a concrete report: what happened, what went wrong, and what staff should say next time.

## How Often Scout.ai Calls

Starter customers could run one or two calls per month. Growth customers could test weekly across common scenarios. Multi-location businesses could run scheduled calls by location, scenario, or employee training focus.

## Ethics Answer

Scout.ai is meant for owners testing their own businesses, not spying on competitors. The product should include clear account ownership, responsible recording/transcription settings based on local law, and customer-configurable disclosure/compliance behavior.

## What Makes It Multimodal

Scout.ai combines voice interaction, transcript processing, structured data storage, and visual dashboard reporting. The product starts with a live voice conversation and ends with a structured coaching interface.

## Sponsor Fit

Vapi powers the live customer-style phone call. Nebius powers scenario generation and transcript reasoning. Insforge stores the workflow data so the owner can review calls, reports, scores, and recommendations over time.

## Likely Judge Questions

### What is the wedge?

Start with phone-dependent local businesses where one missed booking can be worth real money: med spas, salons, dentists, auto shops, repair shops, and restaurants.

### Why not just record calls?

Recordings tell owners what happened only if someone has time to listen. Scout.ai creates the call, analyzes it, and turns it into specific coaching.

### What is the first paid plan?

A simple starter plan with monthly mystery calls, transcripts, scoring, and staff scripts. Higher tiers add weekly calls, more scenarios, trend tracking, and multi-location reporting.
