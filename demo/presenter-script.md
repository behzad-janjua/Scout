# Scout.ai 3-Minute Presenter Script

## Opening

Local businesses spend real money to make the phone ring, but they often have no idea whether those calls turn into customers. Scout.ai is an AI secret shopper for phone calls. It calls your business like a real customer, captures the transcript, scores the experience, and shows exactly which sentences lost the sale.

## Demo

Here I am creating a test for a bike shop. The scenario is simple: a customer calls about e-bike repair and wants to know if they can bring the bike in this week.

Scout.ai uses Vapi to place the mystery shopper call. The voice agent behaves like a normal customer: polite, brief, and focused on one realistic buying question.

After the call, the transcript is saved. This is the key moment: the caller asks, "Could I bring it in this week?" That is high buying intent. But the employee says, "You can just bring it in sometime."

That sentence is the revenue leak. It answers the question, but it does not book the customer, capture their phone number, or give them a clear next step.

Now Nebius analyzes the transcript. The report gives an overall score, the outcome, the customer intent, and the exact worst sentences. It also explains why each sentence hurt conversion and suggests a better version.

Here the better response is: "We have openings this afternoon and tomorrow morning. Would you like me to reserve a repair slot?"

The checklist shows what was missed: no name, no phone number, no booking, no clear next step. Finally, Scout.ai gives the owner a staff script they can use in training.
