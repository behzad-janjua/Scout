# Scout.ai 3-Minute Presenter Script

## Opening

Local businesses spend real money to make the phone ring, but they often have no idea whether those calls turn into customers. Scout.ai is an AI secret shopper for phone calls. It calls your business like a real customer, captures the transcript, scores the experience, and shows exactly which sentences lost the sale.

## Demo

Here I am creating a test for a bike shop. The scenario is simple: an angry customer bought a bike two weeks ago, the rear tire keeps going flat, and they want to know what the shop will do about it.

Scout.ai uses Vapi to place the mystery shopper call. The voice agent starts frustrated but not abusive, then tests whether the employee can calm the customer down, take ownership, and create a concrete next step.

After the call, the transcript is saved. This is the key moment: the caller says the bike keeps going flat, and the employee answers, "Uh, I mean flats happen. We can't really know what caused it."

That sentence is the revenue leak. It may be technically true, but it sounds defensive before the customer feels heard. It does not apologize, take ownership, book an inspection, or capture a phone number.

Now Nebius analyzes the transcript. The report gives an overall score, the outcome, the customer intent, and the exact worst sentences. It also explains why each sentence hurt conversion and suggests a better version.

Here the better response is: "I'm sorry that happened, especially after you just bought it. Let's get it checked and see what is causing the flat."

The checklist shows what was missed: no name, no phone number, no booking, no clear next step. Finally, Scout.ai gives the owner a staff script they can use in training.
